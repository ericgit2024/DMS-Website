Landslide Disaster Information

Learn about landslide risks, preparedness, and safety measures to protect yourself and your community.

  • Before a Landslide
  • During a Landslide
  • After a Landslide
  • Emergency Contacts

Before a Landslide

  • Be aware of areas prone to landslides, especially after heavy rainfall.
  • Listen to local warnings and weather reports.
  • Prepare an emergency kit and evacuation plan.
  • Strengthen slopes and drainage around your home if possible.

During a Landslide

  • Move to higher ground immediately if you suspect a landslide is occurring.
  • Avoid river valleys and low-lying areas.
  • Stay alert for unusual sounds, such as cracking trees or rocks colliding.
  • Follow evacuation orders from authorities.

After a Landslide

  • Stay away from the slide area; further slides may occur.
  • Check for injured or trapped people near the slide, without entering the direct slide area.
  • Report broken utility lines to authorities.
  • Listen to local news for updates and instructions.
