Cyclone Disaster Information

Learn about cyclone safety measures and what to do before, during, and after a cyclone.

  • Before a Cyclone
  • During a Cyclone
  • After a Cyclone

Before a Cyclone

  • Monitor weather updates and cyclone warnings.
  • Secure loose objects and reinforce your home.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials.
  • Know your nearest cyclone shelter and evacuation route.

During a Cyclone

  • Stay indoors, away from windows and glass doors.
  • Listen to official instructions and alerts.
  • Do not venture outside until authorities declare it safe.

After a Cyclone

  • Beware of fallen power lines and damaged structures.
  • Check for injuries and provide first aid if needed.
  • Report hazards to authorities.

For more information, visit the Kerala State Disaster Management Authority.
