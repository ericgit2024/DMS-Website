Donate to Kerala Flood Relief

Your contribution can make a significant difference in the lives of those affected by floods in Kerala. All donations go directly to the Chief Minister's Distress Relief Fund.

Chief Minister's Distress Relief Fund (CMDRF)

The Chief Minister's Distress Relief Fund (CMDRF) accepts voluntary contributions from individuals, organizations, and institutions. All contributions to CMDRF are exempted from Income Tax under section 80(G).

100% of your donation goes directly to relief efforts. The Kerala government ensures transparent utilization of funds for disaster relief and rehabilitation.
